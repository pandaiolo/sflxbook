Coast to coast : an illustration book about twin cities



THE CONCEPT

The red bridge, hilly streets, cable-cars, the surfing culture, the tech initiatives… Have you ever noticed how familiar Lisbon and San Francisco are? After living in Lisboa for several years, we truly have the feeling that the Portuguese capital is the European sister of Californian city San Francisco.

Hence our project: putting the two cities side by side to highlight both their similarities and contrasts. The result is an artbook aiming at offering a new look at two fascinating cities, through colorful, minimalist and unique designs.
